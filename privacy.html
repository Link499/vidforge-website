Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including: account credentials (email, password), payment information processed through our payment provider, and content you upload (text prompts, images, video, audio files). We also automatically collect usage data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and interaction logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: provide and maintain the Service, process payments, generate videos based on your inputs, communicate service updates and support, detect and prevent fraud or abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. User-generated content is retained for 30 days after generation, after which it is automatically deleted from our servers unless you choose to save it.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for: payment processing (Airwallex, PayPal), cloud infrastructure hosting, and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, export your data in a portable format, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vidforge.ai.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

The Service is operated from China. If you are accessing the Service from outside China, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in China. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through the Service at least 30 days before the changes take effect.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@vidforge.ai.
